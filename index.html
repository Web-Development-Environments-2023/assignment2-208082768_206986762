Welcome to SPACESHIP WAR game

introduction
You are on an important mission and need to protect yourself from the evil spaceships. To win you must eliminate all the bad spaceships as fast as possible. Note that you are only allowed 3 disqualifications.

Rules
 - To move with the spaceship, use the arrow keys.
 - to shoot press the spacebar.
 - Note that the game time is 2 minutes, you can extend it through the settings screen.

Additional functionality
 - You can move with the mouse, and shoot with the left mouse button (see this option in thesettings)
 - In addition, you can combine in the game a diagonal movement of all components,
 including your movement, the opponents, and even your shots! You can shoot to the sides using the v and b keys.
 (The bonus function in the assignment)

Good luck!
